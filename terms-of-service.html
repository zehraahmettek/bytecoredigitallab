1.1 Agreement Scope

This agreement is the legal agreement between us and you regarding usage of all application products and services (collectively "Services"). It applies globally and aligns with App Store/Google Play terms, monetization platform rules, and applicable laws including but not limited to cybersecurity, e-commerce, and consumer protection requirements in relevant jurisdictions.

By downloading, installing, or using any Application Product, you confirm that you have fully read, understood, and voluntarily accepted this User Service Agreement, the Privacy Policy, and additional in-app rules (including ad rules and in-app purchase rules). We may update this agreement based on legal updates, platform policy changes, monetization partner requirements, service optimization, or regulatory developments. Updates will be announced by popup, push notification, or in-app notice. Continued use means acceptance of updated terms.

II. Service Content and Scope

2.1 Core Services

We provide complete functional services for local utility and efficiency scenarios and continuously optimize stability, fluency, and quality.

2.2 Monetization-Related Services

  • IAA Advertising Services: compliant ad display such as app open ads, interstitial ads, banner ads, and rewarded video ads. Ad content is supplied by compliant third-party platforms and subject to review under legal and store policy requirements. Users may have ad personalization controls where required; disabling certain ad behaviors may affect availability of some free features.

Purchases are processed through App Store/Google Play or relevant compliant channels. Payment and refunds follow the corresponding platform policy framework. We do not directly process sensitive card credentials where official billing channels are required.

2.3 Additional Services

Additional services may include customer support, issue troubleshooting, version updates, feature optimization, and product usage guidance.

2.4 Service Limitations

We may temporarily restrict or suspend part of Services due to server load, abnormal user behavior, compliance checks, legal obligations, or regional policy constraints. We will notify users where reasonably possible and protect legitimate interests accordingly.
